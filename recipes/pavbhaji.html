Pavbhaji

Description

Pav bhaji is a popular Indian street food consisting of a thick vegetable curry (bhaji) served with toasted or buttered soft white bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is a blend of mashed vegetables, typically including potatoes, tomatoes, peas, and other vegetables, cooked with a special blend of spices. It's known for its bold flavors, comforting texture, and vibrant colors.

Ingredients

  • Peeled 300 grams of potatoes
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • Rinsed half to 3/4th cup green peas
  • Butter
  • Pavbhaji masala
  • Pav(dinner rolls/ bread rolls)

Steps

  1. Add potatoes cauliflower, peas and carrots to a pressure cooker
  2. Pour one and half cups of water
  3. Cook for one to two whistles on a medium flame
  4. Mash the veggies inside the pressure cooker
  5. Heat one tablespoon butter and one tablespoon oil in a pan.
  6. Add 3/4 to one cup chopped onions. Saute till they turn translucent.
  7. Add half cup chopped bell pepper one cup chopped tomatoes and one teaspoon salt, fry till tomatoes turn mushy, soft and pulpy.
  8. Add chili powder and pav bhaji masala powder
  9. Mix well and fry for two to three minutes.
  10. Add the mashed veggies and add some more water to keep consistency
  11. Mix well
  12. Toast pav
  13. Enjoy your meal
